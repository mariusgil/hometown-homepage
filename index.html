Visit Benidorm

Enjoy a beautiful beach, amazing food and connecting with nature, in the Spanish seaside.

Top three activities to do in Benidorm

Swim with dolphins!

Mundomar is an animal theme park where you can swim with dolphins, sea lions and have lots of fun!

Enjoy some churros!

Take a seat at any cafe with a sea-view and enjoy this amazing spanish dessert.

Play beach volleyball!

Feeling sporty? Grab your swimming shorts and join the friendly people of Benidorm in a match of volleyball!
